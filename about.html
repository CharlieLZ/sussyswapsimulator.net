Sussy Swap Simulator was born from the recognition that the cryptocurrency trading landscape needed a better educational foundation. Our founding team, comprised of experienced blockchain developers, financial educators, and DeFi protocol architects, came together with a shared vision: create a platform where anyone could learn cryptocurrency trading safely and effectively.
